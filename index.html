About Us

Godara Farmhouse is a peaceful countryside stay surrounded by open fields and village life. It is designed for families and friends who want to relax away from city noise and enjoy fresh air, homemade food and simple living.

Our farmhouse can host small gatherings, weekend stays, photoshoots and family functions. With open lawns, comfortable rooms and a warm welcome, Godara Farmhouse is a cosy place to recharge yourself.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out timings?

Check-in is usually after 12:00 PM and check-out is before 11:00 AM. Timings can be adjusted a little on request.

Is food available at Godara Farmhouse?

Yes, we provide homemade vegetarian food with local village taste. You can also request special dishes in advance.

Do you allow family functions or small events?

Yes, we allow small family gatherings, birthday parties, and other events. Please contact us for details and charges.

Is parking available at the farmhouse?

Yes, we have free parking space available for guests inside the property.

Is the farmhouse suitable for kids and elderly people?

Yes, the farmhouse is family-friendly with open space for kids and a calm, peaceful environment for elders.
